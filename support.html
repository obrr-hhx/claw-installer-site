常见问题

Q: 如何安装 OpenClaw？

打开 Claw Installer，按照引导页面的步骤，将命令复制到终端中执行即可。

Q: 安装时需要输入密码？

部分步骤需要管理员权限（sudo），输入你的 Mac 登录密码即可。输入时密码不会显示，这是正常的。

Q: 提示"无法连接 OpenClaw"怎么办？

请确认已在终端中执行启动命令，并保持终端窗口打开。OpenClaw 需要在后台持续运行。

Q: 如何配置 AI 模型？

在 App 设置中选择你的 AI 提供商（如 OpenAI、Anthropic 等），填入对应的 API Key 即可。

FAQ

Q: How do I install OpenClaw?

Open Claw Installer and follow the guided setup. Copy the commands and run them in Terminal.

Q: Why does it ask for my password?

Some steps require administrator privileges (sudo). Enter your Mac login password. It won't be displayed as you type — this is normal.

Q: "Cannot connect to OpenClaw" — what should I do?

Make sure you've run the start command in Terminal and keep the Terminal window open. OpenClaw needs to keep running in the background.

Q: How do I configure AI models?

Go to Settings, choose your AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.), and enter your API key.
